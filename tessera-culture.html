Tessera™  ·  Cultural intelligence  ·  System level

The people behind every decision

Cultural context is not a feature of the training module or the communications workstream. It is the foundation every design decision in every unit is built on. This module is the source. Every other unit in Tessera™ draws from what is captured here.

The organising principle: the project is the context. The person is the center. Every recommendation this system generates — every communication, every learning experience, every leadership action, every readiness measure — starts with a specific human being and their cultural context, and works outward from there.
