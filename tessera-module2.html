Layer 3 — Practitioner  ·  Full debrief  ·  Within 60 minutes
1
Emotional texture
"What did it feel like for them to come to work right now?"
Not what they said — what you felt from them. Tired, energised, guarded, burned out, quietly hopeful?
2
Change fingerprint
"How does this person handle change — and what does that look like for this change specifically?"
Not whether they resist or embrace change in general — what does their particular version look like here?
3
What makes them tick
"What did you learn about this person that has nothing to do with work — and everything to do with who they are?"
What do they care about? What gives them energy outside the org chart? This is where Brown's belonging principle lives.
The magic tick
"Did you find the frame that made this change land differently — the analogy, the connection, the reference point that made it suddenly real and relevant?"
If you found it, capture it exactly. If not yet, flag what you're still looking for.
Magic tick found
Not what the project plan says their need is. What you actually heard underneath the conversation.
