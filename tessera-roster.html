What to bring
Any employee data file works — Excel, CSV, or a system export. Org charts exported to spreadsheet format are fine. You do not need every field — import what you have and Tessera will flag what is missing. The data is never fully accurate and that is expected. You will be able to update, add, and remove records throughout the engagement.

Tell Tessera which column is which
Match your file's columns to Tessera's fields. Required fields must be mapped — optional fields add depth to the analysis. If a field does not exist in your file, leave it unmapped and Tessera will flag the gap.
Data accuracy note
Employee data is rarely fully accurate. Tessera will flag common issues — missing managers, duplicate entries, inconsistent department names. You can correct these in the next step or note them for later. The roster updates throughout the engagement as people join, leave, or change roles.
